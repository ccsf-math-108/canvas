Lesson Quizzes

Lesson quizzes are intended to help you reflect on the most recent lesson materials. You can find the answers to these quiz questions directly in the textbook. These quizzes are checking for your ability to recall information rather than testing your understanding of the material.

  Instructions

  1. Review the quiz settings at the top of this page to get an initial understanding of the quiz format and restraints.
  2. Open the relevant lesson material.
  3. Click the Take the Quiz button on this page to begin the quiz.
  4. Once you are finished responding to the questions, click the Submit Quiz button to submit your quiz. If the deadline has ended, then the quiz will be automatically saved and submitted for you. If there is no time limit for the quiz, then you can close the quiz and resume it before the deadline.
