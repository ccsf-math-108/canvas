Discussions

Prepare for the assignments in this course by working through the course content with your classmates. You will collaborate together in real-time with a small group of your fellow students to discuss and respond to a collection of questions. The solutions to these questions will be covered during the discussion meeting of the course. These participatory activities are a great way to bridge the content you gather from the lectures and textbook material with the applications of that content in the notebook-based assignments and exams.

  Instructions

  1. Fill out your copy of the presented worksheet. There is a link below to view a Google Docs version of the worksheet, if you are not using the handout. You'll need to Make a copy of the Google Doc before you can edit your own copy.
  2. Share your responses with the class during the instructor-led discussion.
  3. Show the instructor your completed worksheet for the participation score.
Open Discussion Worksheet in Google Docs

  Assignment Scoring

Discussions will be scored based on your participation. These activities will be scored as Complete (5 points) or Incomplete (0 points). Filling out your copy of the presented worksheet with a reasonable attempt at resolving the questions, generally engaging with the discussion, and presenting that completed worksheet to the instructor will earn a Complete score. Missing or not engaging with the Discussion coincides with an Incomplete score.
