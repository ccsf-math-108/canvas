Lab Assignments

Lab assignments are assigned to help you develop skills with computational and inferential concepts by utilizing Python in a Jupyter notebook environment. You will find that these assignments focus mostly on the content from the most recent learning material, but sometimes there will be opportunities to preview future topics. An auto-grader is used in the lab notebook to help you with the correctness of your code. Your instructor, TA, tutors, etc. are here to help you with both your answers to the coding questions and open-response questions. These labs are here for you to learn from and are not a test of your knowledge!

  Instructions

  1. Open the lab Jupyter notebook in the CCSF JupyterHub.
    Open Lab in JupyterHub
  2. In the lab notebook, follow the directions as you work to respond to all of the coding and open-response questions in the notebook.
  3. Once you are finished, complete the instructions near the bottom of the notebook to download an HTML file that you will use to submit to this assignment page.
  4. When you are ready to submit the assignment, click the Start Assignment button on this Canvas assignment page and upload the HTML file that you downloaded.

  Support

  • If you need to log in to the JupterHub, you will need to use your @mail.ccsf.edu Google account. The password for that account is in your Student Profile in case you do not know it.
  • You might want to use the course Python Reference as you work on this assignment.
  • Check out the Canvas Student Guide on Assignments for a starting place in getting support specifically with Canvas Assignments.

  Scoring

We will score your Lab assignment as Complete or Incomplete based on your participation within 24 hours of the posted Available Until date. Notice that you can find the assignment point value on this Assignment page. 

  • If you submit a notebook HTML file showing that you provided a reasonable response to approximately 75% or more of the assigned tasks, we will score it as Complete.
  • Otherwise, we will score it as Incomplete.

Work submitted after the Due date and before the Available Until date will be marked as LATE by Canvas. This marking is done automatically by Canvas, and it will not impact your grade.
